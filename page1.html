Brief Description

  • Bridge between capacitors and battery
  • Gives high power starts to vehicles
  • Reduces conventional battery usage
  • High charging rate

Advantages

  • High power density
  • Long cycle life
  • High current discharge ability
  • High durability to temperature variations

Applications

  • Motor starter for large engines in tanks and submarines
  • With RBS it can reduce a considerable part of battery usage.
